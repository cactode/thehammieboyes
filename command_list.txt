Directions: (all directions are expected to be immediately followed by a number specifying the distance it should go e.g. d40)
	l - left
	r - right
	u - up
	d - down
	a# - angle (letter a followed by a number.  Make sure to put a _ between the two numbers for angle and distance e.g. a30_40)

Commands:
	dip - move crane over to paint and reapply to brush
	move - tells crane to move, must be followed by a ":" and a direction command

Example set of movements:
	move:r40
	move:d30
	move:r2
	move:a35_6
